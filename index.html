What makes $GLC different from other tokens?

$GLC combines the power of blockchain technology with the ancient tradition of candle magic. While other tokens focus solely on financial aspects, $GLC brings a unique cultural and spiritual dimension to cryptocurrency, creating a community bonded by shared belief in prosperity and good fortune.

How can I purchase $GLC tokens?

$GLC can be purchased on Solana decentralized exchanges like Raydium or Orca. Connect your Solana-compatible wallet (like Phantom), swap SOL or other SPL tokens for $GLC using our official contract address (available on our Telegram and Twitter). Always verify the contract address to avoid scams.

How do reflections work?

Every buy and sell transaction incurs a 5% fee that is redistributed to all $GLC holders proportionally to their holdings. This means your $GLC balance grows automatically just by holding tokens in your wallet. The more you hold, the larger your share of the reflections.

Is there a staking platform?
